## Siddharth Aneja - AI Workflows for SEO

### Post 1: Scaling Content with Guardrails
"When scaling with AI, the biggest risk is the 'hallucination' of facts. You need a human-in-the-loop workflow. AI generates the draft, but experts verify the data. This is how you win in 100% AI-generated search results."

### Post 2: Topical Authority vs. Keywords
"In the era of AI Search (SGE/GEO), Google cares more about your 'Topical Authority' than specific keywords. We are moving toward 'Entity-based' SEO where your brand needs to be the definitive source for a whole topic."
